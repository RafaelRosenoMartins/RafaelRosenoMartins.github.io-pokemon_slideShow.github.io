BULBASAUR

Bulbasaur is a Grass/Poison-type Pokémon. He is recognized for having a seed sprouting from his back from birth. This seed grows as Bulbasaur develops, eventually turning into a flower that releases a sweet, calming scent when healthy.

CHARMANDER

Charmander is a Fire-type Pokémon. He is easily recognized by the flame that burns on his tail from the moment he is born. This flame is an indicator of your emotions; when happy, the flame burns brightly.

POKÉMON
WATER
SQUIRTLE

Squirtle is a Water-type Pokémon, recognized by its hard shell that protects its body and its ability to fire powerful jets of water through its shell. It is easily identified by the dark brown shell on its back and its curl-shaped tail.

POKÉMON
ELECTRIC
PIKACHU

Pikachu is an Electric-type Pokémon, known for its cuteness and the ability to generate electricity through its cheeks. Its bright yellow coat and pointed ears are striking features.
